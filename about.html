Discover Goods Way : Innovating Transportation Solutions

RM Steels, established in 2020, is a prominent player in South India’s steel industry. Headquartered in Kerala, we deliver quality products, including iron scrap, MS billets, ingots, and TMT bars, across six states: Maharashtra, Andhra Pradesh, Karnataka, Goa, Tamil Nadu, and Kerala. Our commitment to integrity, quality, and strong partnerships has led to an expanded network with a robust logistics division, ensuring reliable and efficient distribution.

  1. In 2024, RM Steels introduced GoodsWay, a mobile application that revolutionizes logistics in India by digitizing and enhancing the industry.
  2. GoodsWay brings a new level of connectivity to logistics by enabling fleet owners, transporters, and drivers to operate in an integrated digital environment.
  3. GoodsWay facilitates seamless communication and collaboration among fleet owners, transporters, drivers, and businesses, creating an integrated digital platform that streamlines logistics processes.

Our Mission

At RM Steels and GoodsWay, our mission is to drive efficiency, reliability, and growth in logistics, catalyzing India’s economic transformation. Through innovative technology and exceptional customer satisfaction, we redefine logistics, supporting businesses, job seekers, and the supply chain in an ever-evolving global environment. Collaboratively, we shape India’s logistics future.
